We are an indigenous Electrical Engineering firm based in Kaduna, Nigeria, incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 (Reg. No: 7086104). Established in 2013, we provide professional Electrical Engineering and Allied Services, and are registered with Kaduna Electric (KAEDCO) under Reg. No: 00152.
